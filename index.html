About Me

I am a Machine Learning engineer at Apple Zurich, working on various topics in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. Currently, I am focused on 3D Generative Models and Neural Rendering for Digital Humans.

Previously, I pursued my PhD in 2D and 3D Generative Models at the Computer Vision Lab (CVL) at ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Luc Van Gool.
