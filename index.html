About Me

I am a Machine Learning engineer at Apple Zurich. I work on different topics in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. Currently, I focus on Neural Rendering and 3D Generation. Previously, I did my PhD on 2D and 3D Generative Models at the Computer Vision Lab (CVL) at ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Luc Van Gool.

  • Generative Models
  • Neural Rendering
